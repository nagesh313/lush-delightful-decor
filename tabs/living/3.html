Kick back in sophisticated style with the Debbie 3 seater sofa. Wrapped in plush teal velvet, this squared sofa makes an immediate elegant impact in your living room, office, or lounge. Also available in a rich shade of navy or grey.

This soft-to-the-touch sofa is perfect for sinking into during lazy evenings or having a chat and drinks over glamorous weekends.

Complete the look by pairing with a mid-century modern coffee table and matching side tables.
